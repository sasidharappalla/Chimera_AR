## 📖 About The Project

**Chimera AR** is an innovative Mixed Reality (MR) application designed for the Meta Quest headset. Leveraging the power of the **Meta XR SDK** and **MR Utility Kit (MRUK)**, it enables users to seamlessly blend digital art with their physical surroundings. 

Whether you want to paint custom decals on your walls or hang virtual posters of your favorite shows (like Cyberpunk 2077 or Solo Leveling), Chimera AR turns your room into a dynamic, interactive canvas.

### ✨ Key Features
* **🖌️ Dynamic Room Painting:** Use your Quest touch controllers to point, click, and paint anywhere in your physical space.
* **🖼️ Virtual Posters & Decals:** Cycle through a gallery of presets and slap up high-quality images onto your real-world walls.
* **📐 Adjustable Scales:** Quickly change the size of your brush or decals (from 5cm up to 40cm) on the fly.
* **🏠 Room Scanning Integration:** Seamlessly launches the Meta Space Setup to scan your room and map boundaries, walls, and furniture.
* **🎮 Intuitive Controls:** Includes a continuous painting mode, haptic feedback, and an undo system for absolute creative freedom.

## 🚀 Getting Started

To get a local copy up and running, follow these simple steps.

### Prerequisites
* Unity 2022.3 (or later) with Android Build Support.
* A Meta Quest headset (Quest 2, Pro, or 3) with Developer Mode enabled.

### Installation
1. **Clone the repository**
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/sasidharappalla/ChimeraAR.git
   ```
2. **Open the project** in Unity Hub.
3. Ensure the **Target Platform** is set to **Android**.
4. Connect your Meta Quest and click **Build and Run** (`Ctrl+B` or `Cmd+B`).

## 🎮 How to Play

* **Left Controller (`X` Button):** 
  * `X Button` - Clear all paint and decals.
  * `Index Trigger (Left)` - Toggle continuous painting mode on/off.
* **Right Controller (Painting Tool):**
  * `Index Trigger (Right)` - Paint a decal, select a UI option, or interact.
  * `Grip Trigger (Right)` - Undo the last painted object.
* **In-Game Menus:** Use your laser pointer to scan the room, change colors, adjust brush sizes, or switch to your poster gallery!
